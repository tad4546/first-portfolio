Kenneth Paul Block

The man who changed car culture.

A short timeline of Ken's life.

  • 1967

    Born in Long Beach, Carlifonia, USA.

  • 2005

    Began his National rallying career with the Vermont SportsCar team.

  • 2006

    He competed in the first-ever X Games rally event at X Games XII and ended up finishing third to take the bronze.

  • 2007

    Block competed in the X Games XIII rally event, where he placed second overall and won a silver medal.

  • 2008

    Block gained his first Canadian rally win at the Canadian Rally Championship.

  • 2009

    Block appeared in a segment of motoring show Top Gear

  • 2010

    Block ended his partnership with Subaru, and joined Ford to compete part-time at the World Rally Championship for Monster World Rally Team.

  • 2011

    Block and his co-driver Alex Gelsomino were taken to hospital, having rolled their Ford Fiesta RS WRC during the shakedown stage of the 2011 Rally de Portugal.

  • 2013

    On his first race of 2013 season, he finished top-ten in the Mexican rally.

  • 2014

    He participated in the WRC located in Catalunya, Spain, and finished in 12th place.

  • 2023

    Dies aged 55.
